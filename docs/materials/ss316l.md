# Stainless Steel 316L (SS316L)

Austenitic chromium-nickel-molybdenum stainless steel. Core LPBF material with balanced ductility and corrosion resistance.

## Key Physical Constants
| Property | Value | Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Solidus Temperature ($T_{sol}$)** | 1380 °C (1653 K) | [1] |
| **Liquidus Temperature ($T_{liq}$)** | 1400 °C (1673 K) | [1] |
| **Latent Heat of Fusion ($L_f$)** | 290 J/g | [1] |
| **Reference Density ($\rho_{RT}$)**| 7950 kg/m³ | [1] |

## Simulation Assumptions & Extrapolation
*   **Solid Phase ($T < T_{sol}$)**: Temperature-dependent properties based on Mills (2002) functional fits.
*   **Liquid Phase ($T > T_{liq}$)**: Conductivity and Specific Heat are assumed constant at the liquidus value. Density continues to decrease according to the fit.
*   **Phase Transition**: Latent heat is handled via the effective heat capacity method over the $(T_{sol}, T_{liq})$ range.
